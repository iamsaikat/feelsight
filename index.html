Privacy Policy for FeelSight

This Privacy Policy describes how FeelSight ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent approach to data handling.

1. Information We Collect

We want to be clear: FeelSight does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other data that could identify you. Our focus is on providing a functional tool without compromising your personal privacy.

The App operates primarily by processing data locally on your device.

2. Permissions

Android Camera Permission (`android.permission.CAMERA`)

Our application requires access to your device's camera. This permission is fundamental for the core functionality of the FeelSight app. The camera is used exclusively for:

  • Facial Expression Analysis: The app utilizes the camera to capture real-time video streams of your face. This video stream is processed *locally on your device* to detect and analyze your facial expressions and associated mood.
  • Real-time Feedback: The analysis of your facial expressions provides real-time feedback within the app, helping you to understand and track your emotional states.

Important: We want to assure you that no images or video data from your camera are ever stored, transmitted, or shared with us or any third party. All camera processing for facial expression analysis occurs entirely on your device, and the data is immediately discarded after processing. Your privacy is paramount, and this permission is used solely to enable the app's intended functionality.

Other Permissions

Based on the `AndroidManifest.xml`, the app also requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • `android.permission.INTERNET`: Used for general internet access, potentially for updates or external resources not related to personal data.
  • `android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` and `android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`: These may be used for storing app-related data locally or managing app resources. No personal data is stored using these permissions.
  • `android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`: Allows the app to start certain services (e.g., notifications) after the device reboots.
  • `android.permission.VIBRATE`: Used for haptic feedback, typically for notifications or user interactions.
  • `android.permission.WAKE_LOCK`: Prevents the device from sleeping when certain tasks (e.g., background processing for notifications) are active.
  • `android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS`: Allows the app to send notifications to the user (Android 13+).
  • `android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`: Used for scheduling precise alarms, often in conjunction with notifications.
  • `android.permission.USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT`: Allows the app to use full-screen intents, typically for high-priority notifications.
  • `android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE`: Allows the app to run services in the foreground, essential for continuous operations like background notifications.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit your personal data.
